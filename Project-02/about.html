About

I am a student learning the foundations of web design and front-end development. My current focus is on writing clean HTML, crafting accessible layouts with CSS, and understanding how typography and color shape the way people experience a page.

Outside of coursework, I enjoy exploring digital tools, experimenting with small interface ideas, and finding inspiration in games, apps, and websites that balance clarity with personality. My goal is to build a portfolio that reflects both technical skills and a clear design voice.
